Ani: Analytic Epidemiology

Studys the causes of diseases in certain populations. Occasionally involves experimental work on lab rats and other small mammals.

Quiet and well-thought-of by the rest of the group, Ani is sometimes too soft-hearted for the work she does.

{{tabCtrl.indivStudy}}

Koko: Cognitive Robotics

Developing AI and specific-task machinery. Specializes in small robotics.

A chronic prankster, Koko is a brilliant researcher, but her breakthroughs are often offset by procrastination.

{{tabCtrl.indivStudy}}

Meina: Advanced Weapons Development

Focuses primarily on projectiles and explosives, with occasional grants to also develop defensive measures.

Stubborn and a little spoiled, Meina spends more time than most attempting to socialize with the rest of the group.

{{tabCtrl.indivStudy}}

Sirin: Experimental Genetics

Maintains a small zoo of ongoing experiments in various types of gene therapy, many of them for weaponized purposes.

Unintentionally 'creepy', Sirin has an almost fanatical interest in his own work that tends to put off the others.

{{tabCtrl.indivStudy}}

Tourik: Observational Astronomy

Employs the world's most powerful telescope to record observational data about distant spacial bodies.

Old enough to be the grandfather of the group's younger members, Tourik keeps to himself and subsequently not much is known about his actual work.
