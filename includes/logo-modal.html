For Science!

How far are you willing to go for science?

Welcome to the department. You'll be managing a team of scientists, each a specialist in their own field. This is a highly classified testing environment, so your interaction with the outside world will be limited.

Don't let your team step out of line! Keep an eye on their:

  • Happiness: Increase chances for random grants and other special events.
  • Productivity: Work at a steady pace and gradually earn free time.
  • Economy: Stretch the budget and spend less for everyday items.

What, you'd like to actually meet these people? Do that on your own time - this isn't social hour!

Click that Conduct Research button and get to work!
